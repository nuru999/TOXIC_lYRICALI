The Story

Born from the streets, built on raw words.

Toxic Lyrikali is a Nairobi-based artist whose music sits at the crossroads of Hip-Hop, Afro-Trap and raw spoken word. Growing up in the heart of Kenya's capital, he found his voice in the rhythm of the streets — turning everyday stories into anthems that resonate far beyond the city limits.

With a pen sharper than most and a delivery that cuts through noise, Toxic Lyrikali crafts lyrics that are unapologetically honest. His sound blends Swahili and English, carrying the weight of lived experience while remaining refreshingly open about the hustle, the culture, and the vision.

"Music is the only language that needs no translation — I just speak what I feel, and let the beat carry it home."

— Toxic Lyrikali

Beyond the music, Toxic Lyrikali is a storyteller committed to representing Kenyan culture on a global stage. Every track is a chapter, every album a testament to growth, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of authenticity.
