Biography

The Artist

Toxic Lyrikali is a Nairobi-based artist whose music sits at the crossroads of Hip-Hop, Afro-Trap and raw spoken word. Growing up in the heart of Kenya's capital, he found his voice in the rhythm of the streets — turning everyday stories into anthems that resonate far beyond the city limits.

With a pen sharper than most and a delivery that cuts through noise, Toxic Lyrikali crafts lyrics that are unapologetically honest. His sound blends Swahili and English, carrying the weight of lived experience while remaining refreshingly open about the hustle, the culture, and the vision.

Beyond the music, Toxic Lyrikali is a storyteller committed to representing Kenyan culture on a global stage. Every track is a chapter, every album a testament to growth, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of authenticity.

The Journey

From Struggle to Success

Chapter 1: Humble Beginnings

Born in the streets of Nairobi, Toxic Lyrikali discovered his passion for words at a young age. Poetry became his escape, rap became his voice.

Chapter 2: The Grind

Years of writing in notebooks, performing at open mics, and hustling for studio time. Every rejection fueled the fire to push harder.

Chapter 3: First Break

2018 marked the turning point. First tracks dropped, local buzz built, and the underground scene started taking notice of this raw new talent.

Chapter 4: Rising Star

Collaborations with East Africa's finest, growing fanbase, and a sound that transcended borders. The name Toxic Lyrikali began to echo beyond Kenya.

Chapter 5: Legacy Building

Today, Toxic Lyrikali stands as a voice for the voiceless. Multiple albums, countless performances, and a mission to inspire the next generation.
